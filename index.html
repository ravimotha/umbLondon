Who we are

A welcoming space
for the Umbraco community

The London Umbraco Meetup Group is open to anyone interested in the open source .NET CMS Umbraco — whether you're experienced or brand new. Our aim is to create a friendly environment for learning, sharing knowledge, networking, and feeling part of Umbraco's global community.

We meet mostly on the 2nd Thursday of each month, rotating around London venues (often around Old Street). Talks, demos, case studies, socials, summer BBQs, and yes — the infamous Umbraco London Christmas Pub Quiz.
